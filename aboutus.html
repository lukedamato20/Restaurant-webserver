About Us

Run by family for over 50 years , at Restuarant, we wish to offer our personal rendition of Maltesecuisine with an innovative take on traditional cooking methods, fine Maltese produce and the legacy of our shared food heritage, whilst including italian cuisine in our menu. Moreover, we regard it as a personal challenge to help bring about a revival of Maltese cuisine and let its distinctive flavours and particular regional character brighten up the world.
